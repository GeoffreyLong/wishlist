Our mission

We want every child to have access to things that help them grow and learn. Each child should be able to have as many books as they want to read, or as many science kits as they want to explore, or be able to own a scooter or bike to develop into strong, smart, and healthy people.

Unfortunately, not all families can afford to give their children those toys. Through WishList, you can browse through profiles of children who have been nominated to be on the site, and buy them the gifts that help them grow and learn.
